个人空间 / Personal Space

欢迎来到我的个人空间。我目前是厦门大学马来西亚分校（XMUM）数学系的一名学生。建立这个网站的初衷，是希望能有一个纯粹的地方来存放我的摄影作品。对我来说，镜头是观察世界的一种独特方式，它能帮我定格那些生活里稍纵即逝的光影与瞬间。

与此同时，作为一名数学系学生，我也希望在这里记录下自己的学术成长。因此，在这个专栏里，我会定期整理和分享在专业学习中遇到的数学问题、逻辑推导以及一些心得体会。这里不仅有感性的影像记录，也有理性的思维足迹，它们共同构成了我当下的学习与生活状态。

Welcome to my personal space. I am currently a student majoring in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Xiamen University Malaysia (XMUM). The original intention of building this website was to have a dedicated place to store my photographic works. To me, the lens is a unique way of observing the world, helping me freeze those fleeting moments of light and shadow in life.

At the same time, as a mathematics student, I also hope to document my academic growth here. Therefore, in this column, I will organize and share mathematical problems, logical derivations, and insights encountered in my professional studies. This space contains both emotional visual records and rational footprints of thought, which together constitute my current life and academic journey.
